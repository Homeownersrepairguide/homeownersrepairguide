What this disclosure covers

HomeownersRepairGuide.com participates in affiliate marketing programs. This means that when you click certain product links on our site and make a purchase, we may receive a small commission from the retailer or manufacturer — at no additional cost to you.

This disclosure is provided in compliance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials (16 CFR Part 255) and applicable advertising standards.

How we earn revenue

This site earns revenue through two primary channels:

Affiliate partners may include retailers such as Amazon, Home Depot, and specialty tool and building supply companies, as well as direct manufacturer programs. Specific affiliate relationships vary and may change over time.
