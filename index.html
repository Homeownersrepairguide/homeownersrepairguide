Know Before You Start
DIY it — or call a pro?
🔨
Safe to DIY
Tasks most capable homeowners can handle
  • Replacing faucets, showerheads, and supply lines
  • Resetting tripped GFCI outlets and breakers
  • Replacing air filters and thermostat batteries
  • Patching minor drywall holes and hairline cracks
  • Unclogging drains with a plunger or hand auger
  • Replacing light switches and standard outlets
⚠️
Call a Professional
Tasks that require licensed expertise
  • Any work inside your electrical panel or main service
  • Foundation cracks wider than ¼ inch or horizontal cracks
  • Sewer line replacement or major drain excavation
  • Gas line work of any kind — no exceptions
  • HVAC refrigerant handling (requires EPA certification)
  • Structural wall removal or load-bearing modifications
