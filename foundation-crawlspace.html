Crawlspace Problem Guides

Crawlspace moisture problems are often invisible until damage is significant. Musty smells, soft floors, and high indoor humidity are the most common first signs. Use the symptom pattern to find your article.

⚠ Moisture Risk
Why Your Crawlspace Is Damp
A damp crawlspace creates ideal conditions for mold, wood rot, and pest infestation. The moisture source — groundwater, condensation, or intrusion — determines whether you need drainage, encapsulation, or ventilation changes.

⚠ Air Quality
Why Your Crawlspace Smells Musty
A musty smell rising from a crawlspace into living areas signals mold or organic decay below — driven by moisture that has gone unaddressed. Stack effect pulls crawlspace air directly into your home.
