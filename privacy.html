HomeownersRepairGuide.com ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our site, how we use it, and your choices regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly: When you subscribe to our newsletter or contact us, we collect your email address and any other information you choose to provide.

Information collected automatically: When you visit the site, we and our third-party partners automatically collect certain technical information, including your IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring URLs. This information is collected through cookies and similar tracking technologies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send newsletters and editorial updates you have subscribed to
  • Respond to your messages and questions
  • Analyze site traffic and improve our content and user experience
  • Serve relevant advertising through our advertising partners
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for analytics, advertising, and functionality purposes. These include:

  • Analytics cookies (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use the site
  • Advertising cookies from our ad network partners to serve relevant ads
  • Functional cookies to remember your preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality. You may also opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information about you:

  • Google Analytics — website traffic analysis
  • Email service provider — newsletter delivery and management
  • Ad networks — display advertising (see Advertising section)

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing their use of your information. We encourage you to review them.

5. Advertising

We display advertising on this site served by third-party ad networks. These networks may use cookies and tracking technologies to serve ads based on your browsing behavior across websites. This is commonly known as interest-based or behavioral advertising.

To opt out of interest-based advertising, you can visit the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page or the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email
  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of interest-based advertising as described above

California residents may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
